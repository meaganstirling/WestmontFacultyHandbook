	\subsection{Internships and Fellowships for Recent Graduates Policy}
		Recently graduated Westmont students are eligible for Westmont internships and fellowships, which must be selected through a defined process that uses established criteria consistent with practices in the field and which must be completed√äwithin one year. The Provost is responsible for establishing such selection protocols to ensure that there is integrity in the process and to avoid conflicts of interest. Awards must be approved by faculty department chairs and the Provost.
